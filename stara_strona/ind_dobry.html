Przykład 2

The new optimized architecture supporting Premier and Select DSL in Poland is based on aggregating DSL connections over the national layer 2 ATM backbone, to a single interconnect point where it is directly delivered to a Cisco PE (7500, ESR10K, ASR1K). Each customer site connection is therefore terminating on the PE allowing activation of all IP VPN features such as CoS profiles and traffic prioritization as well as reporting that are needed to provide Premier DSL services.
